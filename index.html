My name is Andy Gnias and I like to code. I graduated from Drexel in 2016 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering. Immediately thereafter, I took a job in software, and after 6 years in the industry I've almost fully quelled my impostor syndrome.

In my free time, I like to read, play soccer, do CrossFit, and pet my dog.
